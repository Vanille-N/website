Tree Borrows

I have worked at [MPI-SWS, Saarland (Germany)](https://www.mpi-sws.org/) on the [miri](https://github.com/rust-lang/miri) Rust interpreter to develop [Tree Borrows](https://perso.crans.org/vanille/treebor), an aliasing model. Presentations of Tree Borrows have been given:

  • online at [RFMIG](https://rust-formal-methods.github.io/meetings/tree-borrows/),
  • in person to teams from [ETH Zurich](https://ethz.ch/en.html) and [Inria Saclay](https://www.inria.fr/en/inria-saclay-centre).
  • to appear in [PLDI'25](https://pldi25.sigplan.org/program/program-pldi-2025/)

You can look at the [material](https://github.com/vanille-n/tree-beamer), a [recording](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zQ76zLXesxA&t=2381s&pp=ygUMdHJlZSBib3Jyb3dz), and the [paper website](https://plf.inf.ethz.ch/research/pldi25-tree-borrows.html)
